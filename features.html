Custom Cakes
A customised cake gives you the freedom to choose from a variety of designs, flavours, and sizes so that there is something for everyone.Clients can choose from our many flavours and decorative options,we can hand decorate it. Our bakery will create the perfect custom cake or pastry's for your special occasions.
Fresh Supplies
Baking at its core is an “old school” craft, which is part of its appeal. Baking has the power to transport people through time. To keep the product fresh and healthy everyday fresh supplies are needed to be used. So, our bakery always keeps day to day product updated to keep our customers happy and satisfied.
Gift And Loyalty
Loyalty programs offer rewards, discounts, or other special incentives and are designed as a reward for a customer's repeat business. Offering rewards that not only interest individual customers, but also encourage those customers to take specific actions that align with a company's business goals.
Advanced Booking
Advance booking is an arrangement that allows you to book and pay for a product before you arrive, usually at a discounted rate. Advance booking is our new service feature where the clients can book a cake up to 7 days in advance.
